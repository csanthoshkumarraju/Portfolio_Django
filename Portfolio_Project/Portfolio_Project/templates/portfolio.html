About Me

As a Systems Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) with over 2 years of experience, I specialize in delivering enterprise solutions across the manufacturing and distribution domains. Throughout my career, I have been focused on optimizing complex processes, analyzing large datasets, and implementing technical solutions for European clients. In my first project, I worked extensively with Oracle Advanced Pricing to manage and optimize pricing strategies for manufacturing clients. My role involved creating and maintaining price lists, applying pricing modifiers such as discounts, surcharges, and promotional pricing, and managing complex pricing scenarios. I performed detailed data analysis to identify and eliminate pricing discrepancies, prepared data using Excel (Pivot, VLOOKUP) and Python OpenPyxl, and ensured accurate data uploads to Oracle EBS and the database. This hands-on experience enhanced my skills in Oracle SQL, Oracle PL/SQL, and data preparation techniques.

In my second project, I focused on data movement and storage optimization. My responsibilities included transferring files from FileZilla to AWS S3, troubleshooting and resolving issues in the DB and AWS environments, and analyzing data logs to identify root causes of issues. I monitored Apache Airflow Dags, analyzing and resolving failed tasks, and worked with data stored in Databricks SQL. I also handled daily reporting on critical issues and resolved JIRA tickets. This project strengthened my proficiency in AWS, Apache Airflow, Informatica, and JIRA.

In addition to my professional work, I am committed to continuous learning and upskilling. In my personal time, I have developed 3 Django web applications, gaining hands-on experience with full-stack web development. I have also completed over 30 Udemy courses, accumulating more than 170 hours of learning in areas such as Python, Django, cloud technologies, and data analysis. This passion for learning drives me to stay updated on industry trends and improve my technical skills.

Throughout both my professional and personal projects, I have honed my abilities in data analysis, web development, and cloud technologies, with a constant focus on quality, system optimization, and continuous improvement. I thrive in dynamic environments where problem-solving, innovation, and adaptability are key to delivering results. Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering, I am passionate about solving complex business challenges and contributing to innovative solutions in the tech industry.
